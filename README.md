## Problem(s): 
Often times, a queer individual is afraid to express themselves freely and is unsure if there are others like them. Often, this leads towards these indivudals often struggling with mental health or physical health. Additionally, many of these individuals struggle in finding resources that can educate them about queer culture.
## Solution(s):
An online platform that allows users to grow plants by completing self-help tasks while learning about these plants and their place in queer cutlure. Additionally, they can plant these flowers either in their own garden or the community garden to either express themselves or to create a community.


## Implementation:

### Backend:
